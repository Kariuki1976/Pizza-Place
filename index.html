About


Americans eat approximately 100 acres of pizza each day, or 350 slices per second,
36% of all pizza orders want their pizza topped with pepperoni.
Your taste buds are the primary factor in what makes pizza special. Along with sweet, salty, sour and bitter, there is a fifth "taste " called umami.
Umami was pinpointed by a Japanese chemist who took his observations into a lab and determined exactly what "umami " was and how it affected your taste buds.
You'll be shocked to know that the carb-fest that is pizza actually fills you with serotonin
This is partially because when you're craving pizza and that craving is satisfied, your brain produces serotonin.
This is also because carbs are used heavily to make serotonin.
